FAQ

Q: Why do you support USA invasions in Iraq, Afghanistan or (insert X country here) and not Russia's invasion into Ukraine? You're a hypocrite!!

A: I never said I supported any of these invasions. As a matter of fact I did not support any of those wars, and I do not support the Russian invasion into Ukraine.

Q: STFU about Ukraine and write your software.

A: I give away this software for free, and I can support whoever I want to. Feel free to stop using my software or take the source code and change it how you like.

Q: Do you hate Russian people?

A: I don't. I sympathise with the Russians soldiers who have been sent into a war they do not even know the purpose of. I don't blame the Russian people who don't stand up against their government because they are afraid of being beaten up and sent to jail for protesting.

Q: Why are you doing this?

A: I believe parts of the world is so full of misinformation around this conflict, and there is a lot of censorship and various media and information channels being blocked around the world. If I can make just a little change, then it's worth it.

Q: Are you blocking LosslessCut in Russia or X country?

A: I have not blocked and will not be blocking the app to anyone. I just want to help spread the word.
